## Backlog

### P0

- Finish backfilling diffusion milestones for the remaining validated profiles.
- Tighten and normalize any remaining dependency-chain edge cases as new profiles are added.

### P1

- Add evidence packs only when a profile becomes citation-heavy or disputed.
- Keep the canonical dataset and profile corpus synchronized through validation.
- Produce the first comparative analysis artifact after the dataset slice stays stable.

### P2

- Build a notebook, chart set, or memo for diffusion and stacking analysis.
- Evaluate whether the repo ever needs a lightweight review surface.
- Consider a public publication surface only after the research workflow is stable.

## Do Not Do Yet

- Do not add a database or warehouse before the markdown-to-CSV workflow proves insufficient.
- Do not add a new framework or app surface just to feel more production-ready.
- Do not start a scraping or ETL pipeline unless a specific research bottleneck justifies it.
- Do not widen the taxonomy before the inclusion rules stay stable across another round of profiles.
- Do not edit generated CSV output by hand.
- Do not confuse the existence of the compiler with a finished product surface.
